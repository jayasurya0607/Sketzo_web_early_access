Jayasurya, Founder

About the Team

Who are we and what we do?

Hey there! I’m Jayasurya, the founder of Sketzo, and at its core, Sketzo is built by just three regular university students who love engineering and design. Along with me, there’s Sushant, the co-founder, and Samuel, our UI/UX designer. We’re all pursuing Mechanical Engineering. We’re not a big startup team or industry veterans just students who saw a problem we personally faced and decided to solve it. While experimenting with existing AI tools, we noticed a big gap. Many AI systems could generate cad models but not the 2D CAD used in industries, That thought eventually became Sketzo. Sketzo is our attempt to make professional-grade CAD accessible to everyone—and it all started with three students asking a simple question: why not build it ourselves?

Sushant, Co-Founder

Why Text to 2D CAD

The core functionality

I’m Sushant, the co-founder of Sketzo, and this part of the journey comes from asking a very practical question: why does CAD still take so long for simple ideas? As engineering students, we constantly convert rough thoughts into sketches before finally opening CAD software. That gap between an idea in your head and a clean 2D drawing—felt unnecessary. We wondered why describing a component in clear mechanical terms couldn’t directly become a usable CAD file. That’s where Text-to-2D CAD came from.

Why pixel theme game

Hi there, I'm Samuel, the UI/UX designer of Sketzo. Creating a pixel-art aesthetic that feels authentic while remaining functional was one of our biggest design challenges. A unique visual identity that makes Sketzo instantly recognizable.
