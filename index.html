Features

Text to 2D CAD

Describe what you imagine in plain text and Sketzo transforms it into a clean 2D CAD drawing, helping ideas move from words to precise lines effortlessly. No more struggling with complex tools or learning curves‚Äîjust type what you want to create, and watch as our AI-powered system interprets your description and generates accurate technical drawings. Whether it's a simple bracket, a floor plan concept, or a mechanical part, Sketzo bridges the gap between imagination and engineering reality.

Professional CAD Experience

Designed to feel close to real-world CAD workflows, so what you learn and draw actually makes sense beyond the screen. Sketzo isn't just a toy‚Äîit's built with industry standards in mind, featuring proper dimension annotations, layer management, and geometric constraints. The skills you develop here translate directly to professional CAD software like AutoCAD, SolidWorks, or Fusion 360. We believe learning CAD should prepare you for the real world, not lock you into a simplified playground.

Download as DXF

Export your drawings as DXF files, the universal standard for CAD data exchange, ready to use in other CAD software, 3D modeling tools, CNC machines, or laser cutters. Your work isn't trapped in Sketzo‚Äîit's designed to flow seamlessly into your broader design workflow. Open your exports in AutoCAD, import them into Fusion 360, or send them directly to a manufacturing shop. One click, and your creation is ready for the next stage of its journey.

Completely Free

No tokens, no subscriptions, no hidden limits, no "trial expired" surprises‚Äîjust a straightforward, free CAD experience for everyone. We built Sketzo because we believe access to design tools shouldn't depend on your budget. Students, hobbyists, makers, and curious minds deserve the same power that expensive software provides. Every feature is available to everyone, forever. No credit card required, no premium tier waiting to unlock the good stuff. Just pure, unlimited creativity.
